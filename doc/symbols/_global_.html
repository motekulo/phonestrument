SimpleBarSequencerAdaptor()
A simple adaptor that connects a bar sequencer to its associated part. An adaptor is able to convert part information into a format useful for a sequencer. The idea here being that a sequencer is simply a view, and a part a means of storing musical information. The adaptor sits in between
Defined in: simplebarseqadaptor.js.
